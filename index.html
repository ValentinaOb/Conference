About Conference

The conference is organised by the Glushkov Institute of Cybernetics of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine, Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv (Faculty of Computer Science and Cybernetics), National Technical University of Ukraine ‘Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute’, Yuriy Fedkovych Chernivtsi National University (Educational and Research Institute of Physical, Technical and Computer Sciences, Department of Computer Technologies).
The following sections are envisaged:

  • Mathematical problems of control, optimisation, game theory, practical stability and sensitivity
  • Stochastic dynamical systems: theory and applications
  • Software engineering and programming theory
  • Computer systems and components
  • Management and modelling in social and economic systems
  • Advanced information technologies and their applications
  • Self-organised and self-configured computer and cyber-physical systems
  • Computerised information and measurement systems
  • Cyber security
